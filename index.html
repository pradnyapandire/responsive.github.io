Fest dates: 21st-23rd February 2017


Details: Criteria was initiated in 2002 and renamed as ETAMAX in 2010


ETAMAX comes from the notation ηmax which stands for MAXIMUM EFFICIENCY.


ηmax’17 brings to you a new dimension that widens and extends the concept of utilizing your resources to the fullest to attain maximum efficiency.
The festival stretches over THREE days and comprises of workshops and competitions (cultural and technical).
